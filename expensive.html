Expen$ive

Personal Finance App

2021
Product Brand Design System Web

Project Overview

A personal finance app designed to help users track expenses and build better spending habits through accessible design and intuitive user experience. The app focuses on making financial literacy accessible to users with varying abilities and technical comfort levels.

Role

Product Designer & Frontend Developer

Duration

4 months

Team

Solo Project

Tools

Figma, React Native, Firebase

The Challenge

Many personal finance apps are overwhelming and inaccessible, particularly for users who are new to budgeting or have disabilities. Traditional finance apps often use complex terminology and dense interfaces that can be intimidating and exclude users who need financial tools the most.

The Solution

Expen$ive was designed with accessibility and simplicity at its core. The app uses clear, jargon-free language, high contrast colors, and intuitive navigation patterns. Every feature was tested with screen readers and designed to work seamlessly with assistive technologies.

Key Features

  • Voice-activated expense logging for hands-free input
  • High contrast mode and customizable text sizes
  • Simple categorization with visual icons and clear labels
  • Gentle budget notifications without financial shame
  • Progress celebrations to encourage positive habits
  • Comprehensive keyboard navigation support

Design Process

The design process involved extensive user research with individuals from diverse backgrounds, including users with visual impairments, motor disabilities, and varying levels of financial literacy. Each design decision was validated through accessibility testing and user feedback sessions.

Impact

User testing showed that 89% of participants found the app easier to use than their current finance tracking method. The app received a 4.8/5 accessibility rating from assistive technology users and was featured in several accessibility design showcases.
