About Me

I’m Kathryn Jordan, an accessibility engineer and educator with a background in front-end development, UX/UI design, and technical training.

I work with teams who want accessibility to be part of how they design and build, not something added at the end.

Through Access Patterns, I offer:

  • Accessibility audits with implementation guidance
  • Team training for designers, engineers, and content creators
  • Design-system and workflow accessibility support
  • Ongoing advisory and embedded accessibility leadership

If you’re interested in working together, you can learn more about my approach or get in touch.
